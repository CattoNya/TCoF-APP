TAROT CARDS OF FATE

Rules

  • The game is 2 player based and consists of a total of 3 rounds.
  • Each round, both players have to pick one random card out of the 7 randomly generated.
  • If the card is in reversed position, minus the points on the card, otherwise plus the points.
  • Throughout the rounds, each player will have a score, the winner being decided by the higher absolute value of the their score.
